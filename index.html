About Me


I'm a developer who enjoys tackling new challenges and refining solutions. I am passionate about building robust, secure, dynamic applications across the full stack. In order to achieve that, my tools include React/Redux, Node/Express, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. I'm also excited to pick up and learn new tools and languages to experiment with or to accomplish the task at hand.

My background consists of a solid foundation in computer science from the University of California, Davis, and further software engineering experience as a student at Hack Reactor.

When I'm not inside hacking away at the computer, I can be found outside hacking away at portals for the Enlightened on Ingress. I also enjoy catching the latest musicals at the Golden Gate and Orpheum Theatres in SF. Come grab a portal or a show with me.
